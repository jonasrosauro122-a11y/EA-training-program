Executive summary

What the team can expect from this training

The program is designed to produce an EA who can protect a leader’s time, communicate professionally, keep work organized, and support operations without constant direction.

Week 1 outcome

The trainee learns executive communication, inbox triage, scheduling discipline, professionalism, and the core tools used in daily support.

Week 2 outcome

The trainee develops stronger follow-through through task management, meeting support, documents, reporting, and workflow efficiency.

Week 3 outcome

The trainee advances into higher-level support through travel planning, CRM work, executive thinking, decision support, and capstone execution.

Assessment model

How the trainee is evaluated

The assessment is designed to measure real executive support judgment, not only memorization.

Pass / fail scoring guide

This grading structure helps the team decide whether the trainee is ready for live executive support, ready with coaching, or needs retraining.

85%–100% Ready to Deploy
Strong judgment, communication, and execution across the core scenarios.
70%–84% Needs Coaching
Promising performance, but needs reinforcement in specific weak areas before full exposure.
Below 70% Retraining Needed
Should repeat key modules before handling independent executive support.
